About

Starshapes

Starshapes is a relaxing single player puzzle game about drawing star constellations in space. Each puzzle consists of abstract shapes that represent stars. Each starshape has unique properties that affect how it can connect to other starshapes in the puzzle.

Starshapes started out as hobby project meant to simply be a simple portfolio piece for the developer to help find jobs in the games industry. Over time however, the ambitions of the developer grew and so the scope of the project expanded to fit the new vision of a fully fledged puzzle game.

Entire Games

Entire Games is a one-person game development studio based in Denmark, and is owned and operated by Peter Riemer Bjørn. Starshapes is the first game that Entire Games has developed and published on their own.

Peter R. Bjørn

Peter has previously worked on Calcium Chaos by Lunch Money Games and has developed several small student games while studying programming and design at the Dania Games academy in Denmark. Peter’s previous projects are available on their personal Itch.io page. As of writing (May 2024), Peter has been developing Starshapes while studying multimedia design over the course of 2.5 years and is hoping to finish the game before the end of the year.
