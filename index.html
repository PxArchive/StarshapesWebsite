FAQ

Q: Who made Starshapes?

Starshapes was created by Peter Riemer Bjørn under the studio name of “Entire Games”.

Q: How long is Starshapes?

An average playthrough should take you around 5 hours. 100% completing the game may take an additional 3-5 hours.

Q: Is there a Demo?

A free demo of Starshapes will be available on Steam at a future ‘Steam Next Fest’ event.

Q: On which platforms can I play Starshapes?

Starshapes will be available on Steam for Windows on release. More platforms and systems may be supported in the future.

Q: Will there be DLC?

There are no plans for extra downloadable content for Starshapes at this time.
